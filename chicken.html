Chicken Tikka

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken, cut into chunks
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon cumin powder
  • 1 tablespoon coriander powder
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Chopped coriander leaves for garnish
  • Onions and lemon slices for serving

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix yogurt, lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, cumin powder, coriander powder, garam masala, red chili powder, turmeric, and salt to make the marinade.
  2. Add the chicken pieces to the marinade, ensuring they are well coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours (overnight for best results).
  3. Preheat your grill or oven to 200°C (400°F).
  4. Thread the marinated chicken pieces onto skewers.
  5. Brush the chicken with oil and grill for about 15-20 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred at the edges.
  6. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve with onion slices and lemon wedges.

Tips: Marinating overnight enhances the flavor. Serve with green chutney for an authentic taste.
