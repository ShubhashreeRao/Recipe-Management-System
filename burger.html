The Classic Paneer Burger

Ingredients

  • 2 burger buns
  • 1 beef patty
  • 1 slice cheddar cheese
  • 1 tomato (sliced)
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Ketchup and mayonnaise
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Grill the beef patty on medium heat until fully cooked.
  2. Toast the burger buns until golden brown.
  3. Place the cooked patty on the bottom bun.
  4. Add a slice of cheddar cheese on top of the patty.
  5. Layer with tomato slices, lettuce leaves, ketchup, and mayonnaise.
  6. Season with salt and pepper as desired.
  7. Place the top bun to complete your burger.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy your burger!
