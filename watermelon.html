Watermelon Juice

Ingredients

  • 4 cups watermelon (seedless, cubed)
  • 1/2 cup cold water
  • 1-2 teaspoons sugar (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Mint leaves for garnish (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Instructions

  1. Add the cubed watermelon to a blender.
  2. Pour in the cold water and blend until smooth.
  3. Strain the juice through a fine sieve to remove any pulp (optional).
  4. Stir in the lemon juice and sugar (if using).
  5. Pour the watermelon juice into glasses over ice cubes.
  6. Garnish with mint leaves and serve chilled.

Tips: Use chilled watermelon for a refreshing experience. You can also experiment by adding a pinch of salt or a few basil leaves for a unique flavor!
