Blueberry Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • 200g digestive biscuits, crushed
  • 100g butter, melted
  • 250g cream cheese
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup blueberries
  • 2 tablespoons blueberry jam
  • Fresh blueberries for garnish
  • Mint leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Mix the crushed biscuits with melted butter and press it into the base of a springform pan to form the crust. Refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  2. In a bowl, beat the cream cheese, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  3. In a separate bowl, whip the heavy cream until stiff peaks form. Gently fold it into the cream cheese mixture.
  4. Pour the cream cheese filling onto the prepared crust and smooth the top. Refrigerate for 4 hours or overnight.
  5. In a saucepan, heat the blueberries and blueberry jam until the blueberries soften and release their juices. Let it cool.
  6. Spread the blueberry topping over the chilled cheesecake.
  7. Garnish with fresh blueberries and mint leaves if desired. Serve chilled.

Tips: You can substitute blueberries with strawberries for a different flavor! Be sure to chill the cheesecake for the full recommended time for the best texture.
